04How we work

Built to pass scrutiny — yours and the assessor's.

◆

You are the author

You stay substantively involved and own the content. We accelerate the work; we don't ghost-write a bid you can't stand behind.

◆

UKRI AI-policy aligned

UKRI permits AI-assisted preparation with human involvement and disclosure of substantive use. We work inside that policy and help you disclose correctly — it doesn't affect your assessment.

Your data stays private

Company financials and drafts are processed on AI infrastructure with no-training terms. Nothing you share is used to train a model or shared onward.

We sharpen the case for real work. We never invent results, partners, or evidence — the fastest way to fail an assessment and lose a funder's trust.
