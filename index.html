“What is the business need or technological challenge behind your innovation?”
Your draft

Our project is critically important for the UK textile industry. There is a significant gap in the scientific understanding of coating durability, which our innovation will address.

Importance 1.1 — asserted, not evidencedNo market size, no £ figure. Reviewers score down importance claims that rest on assertion alone.
